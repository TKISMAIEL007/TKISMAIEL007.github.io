About Me

Hello, I’m Travis — People know me as a former professional rugby player.

Those close to me know me as a thinker and someone who believes in the power of showing up. I created this space to share stories, insights, and lessons drawn from real, lived experiences. Also, I enjoy writting.

This blog isn’t about perfection. It’s about presence. It’s a place to reflect on growth, courage, vulnerability, and the everyday moments that shape who we are. Whether it's a breakthrough or a breakdown, every experience teaches us something valuable.

My hope is that by sharing my journey, I can inspire you to lean into yours — with honesty, heart, and a little humor.

Thanks for being here.
